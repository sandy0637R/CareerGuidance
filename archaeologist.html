WHAT IS

ARCHAEOLOGY?

Archaeology is the scientific study of human history and prehistory through the excavation and analysis of artifacts, structures, and other physical remains. It involves the systematic investigation of past human cultures by uncovering and interpreting material evidence, such as tools, pottery, buildings, and inscriptions, often buried in the ground.
